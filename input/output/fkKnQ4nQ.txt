HIGHER GRADE
(1949)
GEOGRAPHY
PAPER: 1
1. Select one of the following and discuss in what senses it is, and in what senses it is not, a natural region: The Great Plains of North America from the Gulf of Mexico to the Arctic; The Great Plain of Europe from the North Sea to the Urals; The Midland Valley of Scotland.
2. What are ocean currents? How are they caused? Using the North Atlantic Ocean as an example, draw a sketch map to show the main currents, and indicate their effect on the climates of the bordering lands.
3. The U.S.S.R. is fortunate in her waterways, unfortunate in her seaboards. Expand and discuss this statement.
4. Every city of importance in the north-eastern states of U.S.A. owes its position to the possibilities of water transport. Describe, in the light of this quotation, the sites of four of the main cities of the area. [Not more than two of the cities selected should be on the Atlantic seaboard.]
