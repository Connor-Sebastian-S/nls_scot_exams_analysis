HIGHER GRADE
(1929)
ENGLISH
PAPER: 1
1. Write a composition on any one of the following: (a) Slang—its use and abuse. (b) “Play is a serious amusement.” Taking this as a text, discuss the value of games in general. (c) What is a Gentleman? (d) Are the chances of adventure in the world growing greater or less? (e) What part has Scotland played in the development of the Empire?
