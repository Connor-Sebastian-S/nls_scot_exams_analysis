HIGHER GRADE
(1893)
GEOGRAPHY
PAPER: 1
1. State what important industrial districts in England lie outside Lancashire and Yorkshire, and name the products of each.
2. Draw a map of the Lowlands of Scotland, indicating the coal-fields and the ranges of hills, and marking six towns of commercial, and six of historical, importance.
3. Draw a map of the basin of the Thames, or Yorkshire Ouse, or Tweed, showing the hills that limit or intersect it, and the chief towns.
4. What do you know of the African Lakes, and of the discovery of the sources of the Nile?
5. Write a short account of the physical geography of the Spanish Peninsula.
