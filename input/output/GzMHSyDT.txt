LOWER GRADE
(1893)
GEOGRAPHY
PAPER: 1
1. Describe a coasting voyage from Bristol to London, naming the ports that might be called at, and any products that might be taken on board at each.
2. Draw a map of the basin of the Clyde, or Shannon, or Trent, showing the principal towns.
3. Explain the following, and say where examples of each may be found: canon, oasis, monsoon, trade-wind, delta, estuary, veldt.
4. What States border on the Baltic? What rivers enter it from each? What ports are on its shores, and what exports come from them?
5. Draw a map of New Zealand, showing the principal towns. Give an account of the Maoris.
