HIGHER GRADE
(1949)
ENGLISH
PAPER: 1
1. Write a composition, not exceeding three foolscap pages, on any one of the following subjects:
a. My ambition in life.
b. The arguments both for and against any one of the following: vegetarianism, the prefect system, a universal language.
c. The school debating or literary or dramatic society as an important factor in education.
d. Has the aeroplane been a boon or a bane to mankind?
e. Describe a visit by Sir Roger de Coverley to any one of the following: the skating rink, the agricultural show, the public library, the dentist.
