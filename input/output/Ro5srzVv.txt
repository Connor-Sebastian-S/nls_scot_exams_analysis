HIGHER GRADE
(1939)
ENGLISH
PAPER: 1
1. Write a Composition, not exceeding three foolscap pages in length, on any one of the following subjects: Describe your favourite hobby and state what pleasure and profit you derive from it. Narrate, in the form of a letter to a friend, any adventure which you may have had either on the sea or among the hills. “Sweet are the uses of adversity.” “A room without pictures is like a house without windows.” With this sentence as a text, write on the place of art in the home. What can you say for, and what against, modern methods of advertising? Choose one of these three suppositions and imagine the results: “If Edward II had won the battle of Bannockburn,” “if James IV had won the battle of Flodden,” “if Prince Charles Edward had won the battle of Culloden.”
